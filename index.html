About This Book

This interactive textbook explores the fascinating intersection of artificial intelligence and physical robotics, focusing on humanoid systems that can perceive, think, and act in the real world.

Through animated diagrams, 3D simulations, and hands-on projects, you'll learn how to design, build, and program humanoid robots that exhibit human-like behaviors and capabilities.
